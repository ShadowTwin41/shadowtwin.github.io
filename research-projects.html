Generation of Synthetic Rat Brain MRI scans with a 3D Enhanced Alpha-GAN

Pioneered the first GAN-based approach to generate realistic 3D synthetic rat brain MRI scans, addressing critical data scarcity and ethical (3Rs) challenges in translational neuroscience. Validated model realism using quantitative metrics, a Turing Test, and segmentation applications, demonstrating that synthetic data outperformed conventional data augmentation in improving segmentation model performance.
